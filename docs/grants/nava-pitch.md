# NAVA — Executive Pitch

## One line

NAVA is an open-source, self-hostable platform for building autonomous AI agents that safely operate real
infrastructure — under human control, on any model you choose, inside your own walls.

## The 30-second version

Every AI-agent product today is closed and cloud-only — you ship your data and business logic to someone
else's platform, outside EU jurisdiction, locked to one model vendor. NAVA flips that: an open-source,
multi-tenant control plane where each customer builds their own LLM agents that plan, remember, run on
schedules and events, and act on real systems (Kubernetes, servers) through secure outbound tunnels — no VPN,
no open ports. Risky actions need human approval; unattended runs are read-only by design. Bring your own
model — GPT, Claude, or a local Llama on your own GPUs. Built on the open `kcp` framework, so anyone can
extend it with new "providers." **European digital sovereignty for AI operations.**

---

## What NAVA **IS**

- **Open source & self-hostable** — run it in your own datacenter; audit every line.
- **An autonomous-agent platform** — agents with memory, schedules, heartbeats, event triggers; not one-shot
  chatbots.
- **Model-agnostic** — any OpenAI-compatible or Google model, cloud or local. BYO credentials.
- **Secure-by-design** — trigger-scoped trust (unattended = read-only), human approvals inbox, full audit,
  per-agent budgets.
- **Infrastructure-native** — a real-time GraphQL "digital twin" of your distributed systems; reverse-tunnel
  access with no VPN.
- **An extensible ecosystem** — a `kcp` provider framework; third parties add their own APIs, tools, and UIs
  (agents + app-studio already prove it).
- **Vendor-neutral & international from day one** — OSS by nature, not tied to one market or one supplier.

## What NAVA is **NOT**

- **Not a closed SaaS-only product** — no forced data exfiltration to an external cloud.
- **Not locked to one LLM vendor** — you're never captive to a single model or its pricing.
- **Not a chatbot** — it acts, on a schedule, with tools and consequences, not just replies.
- **Not traditional RPA** — it operates at the API/infrastructure level, not by mimicking mouse-clicks on a UI.
- **Not an ungoverned autonomous agent** — no write actions without policy + human approval; safe against
  prompt injection by design.
- **Not a research project** — applied product development from mature components to TRL 8–9, not R&D.
- **Not market- or region-specific** — international OSS; the EU is the launch anchor, not a boundary.

## Lietuviškai (elevator)

**NAVA** — atvirojo kodo, savarankiškai diegiama platforma autonominiams DI agentams kurti, kurie **saugiai
valdo realią infrastruktūrą** su žmogaus kontrole, su bet kokiu pasirinktu modeliu, **jūsų pačių aplinkoje**.

Skirtingai nuo uždarų, tik debesyje veikiančių DI agentų produktų (kur duomenys ir logika atiduodami išorinei,
ne ES platformai), NAVA yra daugiaklientė valdymo plokštuma, kurioje kiekvienas klientas kuria **savo** LLM
agentus: jie planuoja, turi atmintį, veikia pagal tvarkaraštį ir įvykius, ir atlieka operacijas realiose
sistemose per saugius atvirkštinius tunelius — **be VPN, be atidarytų prievadų**. Rizikingi veiksmai
reikalauja žmogaus patvirtinimo; neprižiūrimi paleidimai — tik skaitymo režimu. **Pasirink savo modelį** (GPT,
Claude ar vietinis Llama). Sukurta ant atviro `kcp` karkaso — **Europos skaitmeninis suverenitetas DI
operacijoms.**

**Kas NAVA yra:** atviras kodas · autonominiai (ne vienkartiniai) agentai · modelių atžvilgiu neutrali · saugi
pagal dizainą · infrastruktūrai gimtoji (skaitmeninis dvynys) · plečiama tiekėjų ekosistema · tarptautinė nuo
pirmos dienos.

**Kas NAVA nėra:** ne uždaras SaaS · ne pririšta prie vieno modelių tiekėjo · ne pokalbių botas · ne tradicinis
RPA · ne nevaldomas agentas · ne mokslinis tyrimas · ne vienai rinkai skirtas įrankis.
